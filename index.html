Curriculum Vitae

About

I received my B.B.A. from Yonsei University and am currently a master’s student in Operations Management at Yonsei, advised by Professor Seung Hwan Jung.

To date, my work has been primarily methodology-driven, and I have explored a range of domains including fulfillment forecasting, government policy, and generative AI. In machine learning, I have contributed to two papers, which led to my service as a reviewer for Computers & Industrial Engineering.

Looking ahead, I hope to develop methodological skills that integrate algorithmic, empirical, and stochastic modeling approaches. I am also envisioning my dissertation as a behavioral study that combines experimental methods with analytical modeling.
